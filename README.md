The project is an AI agent for Mental Health care. A person can text or say his mental problems or his concerns affecting his mind that can be from simple stress related to heavy mentaldistress with suicidal tendencies. The AI agent will clarify it if necessary and give a brief solution to overcome the mental problems. You can approach the agent as an advisor to your official or household tensions. The AI agent will advice a solution only if it is under its control, otherwise it will request to consult a human mental healthcare specialist. It provides polite and soothing words.
